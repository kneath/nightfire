Races

These are all of the races that will eventually or are in Nightfire right now, races in red have been implemented already.
Balanced (Human) – The newbie race. Balanced everything.

Super-Mage (Xorx) – Their advantage lies in using mana for everything. Mana replaces energy for them and all military units are mages…no tech units. Thus, they only need Element-X for construction. Military units will be below average in strength, barracks MUST be built on blessed lands.
Super-Tech (Larxons) – Consumed by science, the Larxons have mastered genetic manipulation. About 2 centuries prior to the present, three Doctors split from the homeworld and started researching the effects of Element-X on different animals. They made amazing breakthroughs and forever changed the Larxon race. The Larxons are now well known for their amazing efficiency. However, in order to reach this efficiency, massive amounts of resources are required.

Speed-Freak (Rekkans) – Very fast yet wasteful race. Has construction/production/training/attacking speed bonus but will waste shit loads of energy. Speed bonuses are to be greater than the red giants speed penalty (since that’s a minor penalty in the first place). Units are somewhat low in price and Element-X cost.

Organized/Religious (Elvars) – While not as powerful in magic as the super-mage race, this race actually has a good army. They will be extremely organized and efficient, allowing very high income since every civilian is filthy rich ;-) Religious people, with minor magic bonuses. Rather pacifist ideology causing insanely high morale loss in battles. Small fraction of military resigns over time.

Evil/Bloodthirsty (Vorlons) – A race actually behind in both technology and magic, but still keeps up with the other races in war. Has reckless ideology and civilians are willing to live in low living conditions to support war. Has VERY powerful military units and higher income after each successful attack. Low research levels and magic penalty. Military tech units are powerful but bulky, so there’ll be an attack speed penalty.

Smart/Small (Ostarians) – Starts out with a few levels of conservation, medicine, and stealth research. Lower construction/production/training/attacking energy costs. Race is physically weak and small, with weak soldiers and civilian health can be hit harder than otherwise.
